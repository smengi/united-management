Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

I am a tenant — how do I submit a maintenance request? +

Submit maintenance requests by emailing management@unitedmanagementholdings.com or by using the Tenant Message Form on the Tenant Resources page. Please include a description of the issue, its location within the property, and the urgency level.

Where do I send my rent payment? +

Payment instructions are provided directly by management and are not published on this website. If you have not received payment instructions or need to confirm current payment details, please contact management at management@unitedmanagementholdings.com.

I am a vendor — how do I coordinate with management? +

Vendors and contractors should contact United Management Holdings LLC by email to coordinate scheduling, scope, and documentation. Please include your company name, license information, and the property or work order reference in your message.

How long does it take to receive a response? +

United Management Holdings LLC typically responds to inquiries within 1–2 business days. For urgent or emergency matters, include "URGENT" in your email subject line to flag the message for priority response.

How do I submit a Certificate of Insurance (COI)? +

Send your Certificate of Insurance directly to management@unitedmanagementholdings.com with your name, business name, and property location in the subject line. Management will confirm receipt and notify you if any additional information is required.

Is space available for lease at Folsom Lake Shopping Center? +

For leasing and space availability inquiries, email management@unitedmanagementholdings.com with your business name, space requirements, and contact information. Management will forward your inquiry to ownership and the property's leasing representative as appropriate.
