Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start managing my property? +

In most cases, we can begin the onboarding process within 48–72 hours of signing our management agreement. We'll handle everything — from marketing your property to coordinating any needed inspections.

Are there any long-term contracts? +

We offer flexible agreements. While we work best with a minimum 6-month commitment, we don't believe in locking owners in. If you're not satisfied, you can cancel with 30 days' written notice.

How are maintenance costs handled? +

You set a maintenance approval threshold (e.g., $300). Repairs under that amount are handled immediately without waiting for your approval. Anything above requires your sign-off before we proceed. All costs are deducted from collected rents and fully itemized on your monthly statement.

When do I receive my rental income? +

Owner disbursements are made between the 10th–15th of each month, after rent has been collected and reconciled. Funds are sent via direct deposit to your bank account.
