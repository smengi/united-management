Tenant Requests & Coordination

Submit maintenance requests, verify payment instructions, send documentation, and coordinate access, directly with United Management Holdings LLC. No account is required.

1 Select Your Property

Which property are you contacting us about?

No account is required to submit a tenant request. Select your property below and choose the type of request you would like to submit.

Management contact: United Management Holdings LLC is the management contact for Folsom Lake Shopping Center. Tenants, vendors, and contractors should direct maintenance, documentation, payment verification, access, and general property matters to management@unitedmanagementholdings.com.

Emergency? Call 911 first.

For fire, active crime, a gas smell, medical emergencies, or any immediate life or safety concern, call 911 first, then notify management. Website forms are reviewed during business hours and are not for life-safety emergencies.

3 Submit Your Request

Tenant Request

Your request goes directly to United Management Holdings LLC management. Responses are provided in writing, typically within 1–2 business days.

Submitting documents? After completing this form, email COIs, photos, or other tenant documents to management@unitedmanagementholdings.com with your business name and property in the subject line.
What to include in a maintenance request

Please include your business name, suite or unit, the exact issue location, a clear description, photos if available, urgency level, permission-to-enter instructions, and the best contact person for follow-up.

Your information is used only to respond to your request. United Management Holdings LLC does not share contact information with third parties.

Your request has been received. United Management Holdings LLC will respond within 1–2 business days. If you are submitting a document, please email it to management@unitedmanagementholdings.com.

Payment Security Notice

UMH does not publish wire, ACH, or bank instructions publicly. Payment instructions are provided directly in writing and should be verified with management before any change.

If you receive unsolicited payment instructions claiming to be from management, do not send funds. Contact management at management@unitedmanagementholdings.com to verify first.
