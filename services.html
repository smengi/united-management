What We Do

Commercial Property Management Services

Organized, practical, and responsive operational support for commercial real estate owners, tenants, and vendors.

United Management Holdings LLC provides management and operational coordination services for commercial real estate assets. The services described below reflect the operational scope of the company's management activities. For questions about a specific property or engagement, please contact management directly.

Service 01

Commercial Property
Management

Day-to-day coordination for commercial properties, including tenant communication, maintenance coordination, vendor management, and operational follow-through. United Management Holdings LLC serves as the primary operational point of contact for the property.

Day-to-Day Operations

Ongoing coordination of property operations, maintenance requests, and routine management activities.

Primary Management Contact

Single point of contact for tenants, vendors, and ownership on property operational matters.

Operational Follow-Through

All matters are tracked, documented, and followed through to resolution.

Scope Includes

  • Tenant communication and correspondence
  • Maintenance request intake and coordination
  • Vendor scheduling and follow-up
  • Property access coordination
  • Operational documentation and record-keeping
  • Routine property inspections (as applicable)

Communication Areas

  • General property inquiries and information
  • Maintenance request coordination
  • Insurance documentation and COI requests
  • Management notices and correspondence
  • Payment-related instructions (provided directly)
  • Lease-related communications (as applicable)

Service 03

Vendor & Maintenance Coordination

Coordination with approved vendors, contractors, and service providers to help keep property operations organized and responsive. United Management Holdings LLC manages vendor relationships on behalf of the property to ensure quality, accountability, and documentation.

Approved Vendor Network

Work with pre-approved, qualified contractors and service providers for property needs.

Maintenance Request Tracking

All maintenance requests are logged, coordinated, and followed up through to completion.

Vendor Documentation

Insurance, licensing, and scope confirmation maintained for all engaged service providers.

Coordination Includes

  • General repairs and preventive maintenance
  • Emergency response coordination
