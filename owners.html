A Dedicated Management Partner

United Management Holdings LLC is a commercial property management company serving affiliated and select third-party ownership groups. UMH handles day-to-day operations, tenant communication, maintenance coordination, vendor management, invoice review, lease administration support, owner reporting, and transition support for newly acquired assets.

The company manages commercial property with organized operations and clear communication, and is set up to take on additional properties over time.

What UMH Handles

Owner Services

Practical, organized management built around the day-to-day realities of operating commercial real estate.

01

Commercial Property Management

Day-to-day coordination of property operations, tenant communication, maintenance, and vendor oversight with reliable follow up.

02

Owner Reporting

Property updates, expense tracking, and reporting that keep owners informed.

03

Tenant Communication

Centralized, documented communication with tenants for questions, requests, notices, and coordination, handled in writing.

04

Vendor Oversight

Coordination of approved vendors and contractors, documentation requirements (COI, license, W-9), scope confirmation, and invoice review.

05

Lease Administration Support

Tracking key lease dates, certificates of insurance, notices, and documentation so nothing critical slips through.

06

CAM / NNN Coordination

Support for common area maintenance and triple-net expense coordination, tenant recovery communication, and operating expense tracking.

07

Capital Improvement Tracking

Tracking capital projects, scope, vendors, and progress so improvements are documented and coordinated alongside daily operations.

08

Property Transition Support

Support during ownership or management transitions, including tenant onboarding, vendor setup, contact updates, and document organization for newly acquired properties.
