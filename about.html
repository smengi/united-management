Who We Are

More Than a
Management Company

United Management was founded with a simple belief: property owners deserve a management partner they can truly trust — one that communicates openly, operates with integrity, and treats every property as their own.

Since 2009, we've grown from managing a handful of local properties to overseeing a diverse portfolio of 500+ residential and commercial units across the region. Our growth is a direct result of the relationships we've built and the results we've delivered.

Today, our team of dedicated property managers, maintenance coordinators, and financial specialists bring the same passion and professionalism to every property in our portfolio — regardless of size.
