About UMH

A Commercial Property Management Company

United Management Holdings LLC is a commercial property management company focused on clear communication, organized property operations, and responsive tenant and vendor coordination.

Who We Are

A Dedicated Management Company

UMH is owner-operated, so tenants, vendors, and owners work directly with management instead of being passed through layers of departments.

The company currently manages Folsom Lake Shopping Center in Folsom, California, and is set up to support additional commercial properties over time.

How UMH Works

What working with UMH looks like day to day.

  • Owner-operated, with direct access to management
  • Set up to manage more than one property
  • Organized property operations
  • Clear, written communication
  • Responsive follow up
  • Serving Folsom and Northern California
How We Operate

What Sets UMH Apart

Centralized Communication

Tenants, vendors, and owners get a single, organized point of contact for each property. Responses are provided in writing to maintain a clear record.

Organized Operations

Requests are received, reviewed, coordinated, and documented from start to finish, with clear records along the way.

Reliable Follow Up

Work is tracked to completion. Owners stay informed, and tenants and vendors know where things stand.

Operating Area

United Management Holdings LLC manages commercial property in Folsom, California and the greater Northern California region, and is set up to support additional properties over time.
