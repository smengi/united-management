About UMH

A Commercial Property Management Company

United Management Holdings LLC is a commercial property management company managing commercial and income-producing real estate for affiliated and select third-party ownership groups across Northern California — built on disciplined operations, organized communication, and accountable follow-through.

Who We Are

A Dedicated Management Company

United Management Holdings LLC is a commercial property management company that manages commercial and income-producing real estate for affiliated and select third-party ownership groups. The company is structured to take on additional properties over time while keeping every asset organized, documented, and accountable.

UMH combines disciplined operations with responsive, professional service — clear communication for tenants and vendors, dependable coordination, and transparent reporting for ownership across Northern California.

How We Operate

What Sets UMH Apart

Centralized Communication

Tenants, vendors, and owners get a single, organized point of contact for each property. Responses are provided in writing to maintain a clear record.

Organized Operations

Requests are received, reviewed, coordinated, and documented from intake through completion — with clear records along the way.

Accountable Follow-Through

Work is tracked to completion. Owners stay informed and tenants and vendors know where things stand.

Operating Area

United Management Holdings LLC provides management and operational coordination support for commercial real estate in Folsom, California and the greater Northern California region. UMH is structured to support affiliated and select third-party ownership groups and additional properties over time.
