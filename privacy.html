Privacy Policy

How United Management Holdings LLC handles information submitted through this website.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026. This policy applies to the United Management Holdings LLC website at unitedmanagementholdings.com.

Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form on this website, we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, company or organization name, and the content of your message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

This website does not use tracking cookies, advertising networks, analytics services that collect personal data, or any third-party data collection tools beyond the contact form processing service (Formspree) described below.

How We Use Your Information

Contact information submitted through this website is used exclusively to respond to your inquiry. United Management Holdings LLC does not sell, rent, trade, or share your contact information with third parties for marketing or any other purpose.

We do not send unsolicited marketing communications. If you contact us, we will respond to your inquiry only.

Contact Form Processing

Contact form submissions on this website are processed through Formspree, a third-party form processing service. By submitting a form, your information is transmitted to Formspree and forwarded to United Management Holdings LLC management. Formspree's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

Alternatively, you may contact management directly by email at management@unitedmanagementholdings.com.

Payment Information Security

Important Payment Notice

United Management Holdings LLC does not publish ACH details, wire transfer instructions, banking information, or any payment-related account numbers on this website. Payment instructions are always provided directly and in writing by United Management Holdings LLC management. If you receive unsolicited payment instructions from any source, contact management to verify before sending any funds.

Data Retention

Contact inquiries submitted through this website are retained only as long as necessary to respond to and document the inquiry. Management correspondence is retained in accordance with standard business record-keeping practices.

External Links

This website may contain links to external websites. United Management Holdings LLC is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites.

Changes to This Policy

United Management Holdings LLC may update this privacy policy from time to time. The effective date above will reflect the date of the most recent update. Continued use of this website following any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, contact United Management Holdings LLC at management@unitedmanagementholdings.com.
