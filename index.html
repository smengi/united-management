Operations

How Management Requests Are Handled

Every request — tenant, vendor, or ownership — is received, reviewed, coordinated, and followed through to completion.

1

Submit Request

Tenants or vendors contact management by email or through the website form, including property location, request type, and relevant details.

2

Management Reviews

United Management Holdings LLC reviews the request and confirms next steps, typically within 1–2 business days. Urgent matters receive priority response.

3

Coordination

If needed, management coordinates with vendors, contractors, ownership, or the relevant documentation — scheduling work, confirming scope, and tracking progress.

4

Follow-Through

Requests are tracked and documented from intake through to completion. Management provides updates and maintains a record of all property-related actions.
